Methodology -
We compare the average trip distance between two groups and to do so, we use t-test for independent samples. The motivation behind this choice is:
- We compare the means of two samples.
- We can use a parametric test as sample size is large enough (so, we don't choose non-parametric test). We could do F-test before t-test, but
- The samples are independent (so, we should not use t-test for paired).
